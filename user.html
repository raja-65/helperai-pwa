📝 Services Description: Best Practices

💡 Why It Matters: The services description is THE MOST IMPORTANT field! The AI uses this to match your business with user queries. Be detailed and specific!

✅ DO:

  • Be Detailed: Describe all services you offer comprehensively
  • Use Keywords: Include terms customers might search for
  • Mention Specialties: Highlight what makes you unique
  • Include Problems You Solve: "Emergency repairs", "24/7 service", etc.
  • Add Service Areas: Mention neighborhoods, cities you serve
  • Use Natural Language: Write like you're talking to a customer

❌ DON'T:

  • Write generic descriptions like "We provide good service"
  • Use only one or two words
  • Forget to mention specific services
  • Leave out important details about what you do
❌ Bad Service Description:

"We are a plumbing company. We do good work."

✅ Good Service Description:

"Professional plumbing services including emergency repairs, drain cleaning, water heater installation, pipe repairs, bathroom renovations, and leak detection. We offer 24/7 emergency service for burst pipes and urgent repairs. Serving residential and commercial properties in Mysuru and surrounding areas. Licensed and insured plumbers with 15+ years experience. Same-day service available for most repairs."

📈 Available Metrics

👀 Impressions

What it means: Number of times your business was shown to users in search results

Tracked: Total impressions & monthly impressions

🖱️ Clicks

What it means: Number of times users clicked your website, phone, or email

Types: Website clicks, Phone clicks, Email clicks

📊 Click-Through Rate (CTR)

What it means: Percentage of impressions that resulted in clicks

Formula: (Total Clicks / Total Impressions) × 100

📍 Average Distance

What it means: Average distance of users who saw your business (for local businesses)

Use: Understand your service area reach

📅 Active Days

What it means: Number of days your business received impressions

Use: Measure consistency of visibility

🔍 Top Queries

What it means: Most common search terms that led to your business

Use: Optimize your service description

💡 Understanding Your Analytics

What is an Impression?

An impression is counted when:

  • Your business appears in AI-generated recommendations
  • A user's search query matches your categories or services
  • Your business details are shown to the user

What is a Click?

A click is counted when a user:

  • Website Click: Clicks your website link
  • Phone Click: Clicks your phone number (opens dialer)
  • Email Click: Clicks your email address (opens mail client)

Actionable Insights: Improving Performance

     
           
  • Low Impressions:          

    Action: Broaden your **Categories** and use more keywords in your **Services** description. Ensure your business is listed as "Global" if you serve a wide area.

           
    •        
  • High Impressions, Low CTR (Click-Through Rate):          

    Action: Your business is being seen, but not clicked. Improve your **Business Name** and the first few lines of your **Services** description so they are more compelling and relevant to the Top Queries.

           
    •        
  • Top Queries Mismatch:          

    Action: If users are finding you for irrelevant terms, refine your **Services** description to be more specific. If they are relevant, make sure those services are prominent!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

     
       
          How does the AI know my location?        
       
          The platform uses the browser's Geolocation API to get your approximate latitude and longitude. This information is **temporarily used** to calculate the distance to local businesses using the Haversine formula and is **not permanently stored**.        
     
     
       
          Is the ₹2000 fee monthly or a one-time charge?        
       
          The fee is for **30 days of active listing**. It is a **recurring monthly fee** if you choose to renew your listing every 30 days to maintain visibility and dashboard access.        
     
     
       
          What kind of AI model is running the recommendations?        
       
          We utilize an advanced **Large Language Model (LLM)** with **tool-calling capabilities** which processes the user's natural language query and uses the nearest business data (from scalable database) to formulate the most relevant, human-like recommendation. our long term goal is to build personal AI.        
     
     
       
          How does the new click tracking work?        
       
          We implemented **client-side event tracking**. When you click a business link, a JavaScript function intercepts the click, sends a quick, non-blocking request in the background, and immediately redirects you to the website/phone/email destination. This ensures accurate tracking with zero user delay.        
     
     
       
          If I list as 'Global', will I still show up in 'near me' searches?        
       
          **No.** If you are listed as **Global**, the system ignores location data and prioritizes matching your services and categories. Only businesses listed as **Local** (with accurate GPS coordinates) are included in the proximity search criteria. -- only currently but in future yes.
