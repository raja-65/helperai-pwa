Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At HelperAI, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our AI-powered business discovery platform.

HelperAI operates as an information center where businesses register to receive AI-powered recommendations to potential customers. We help users discover local businesses while providing businesses with valuable customer insights.

This privacy policy applies to all users of our platform, including customers seeking business recommendations and business owners who register their services with us.

How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Customer Chat Data: 12 months for service improvement, then anonymized
  • Business Information: Until account cancellation plus 30 days for billing purposes
  • Analytics Data: 24 months in aggregated, anonymized form
  • Payment Records: 7 years for tax and legal compliance

Automatic Deletion

We automatically delete personal data when it's no longer needed for the original purpose it was collected. Inactive customer sessions are purged after 12 months, and cancelled business accounts are removed after 30 days.

Right to Be Forgotten

You can request immediate deletion of your personal data at any time. For business accounts, we'll remove your listing from customer recommendations within 24 hours of account cancellation.
